    0 3 — P R O J E C T D I R E C T I O N S

    Focus on technically challenging projects. Three directions worth
    chasing:


    A   Combine ML with LLM applications
        Pair classical machine learning models with LLM-driven systems so each does what it's
        best at.




B A philosophically different take on agents
        Rethink how agents are leveraged. For example: could a company survive if a team worked
        through one shared agent that did most of the work — and the team simply verified it?



    C   One problem, many approaches
        Take a simple problem and attack it from several angles — solving it multiple ways and
        comparing what actually wins.
